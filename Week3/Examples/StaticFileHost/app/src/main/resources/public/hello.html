This is my favorite recipe of all time

And it is ready in only 7 and a half minutes!

Here are the ingrediants list:

  • One box of Kraft Mac and Cheese
  • 2 Table Spoons of Butter
  • 1/3 Cup of Milk
  • 6 Cups of Water

How to cook the mac and cheese

  1. Fill a pot with 6 cups of water
  2. CAREFULLY put the pot on the stove
  3. Put the stove on HIGH to boil the water
  4. After the water is boiling, add the noodles, don't accidenally drop the cheese packet in
  5. Cook the noodles for 7 and a half minutes, and turn off the stove
  6. Drain the noodles, preferably in the sink so you spill water everywhere
  7. Put the noodles back in the pot, and back on the stove
  8. Add the butter,the sauce packet, and milk
  9. Stir until butter is melted
  10. Eat the entire box, you earned it
